Overview of KGRITM

KGR Institute of Technology and Management run by KGR Group of Industries, under the dynamic supervision of Sri Lion Dr. K. Govinda Reddy with a primary purpose of providing the quality education for all. The fact that we believe in ‘fuelling the quest of knowledge’ KGRITM is not only emerging as a leader in the field of Management and pharmaceutical studies in Telangana but also is positioned as the best Professional Educational Institution. Since its inception, it is offering quality education with state-of-the-art facilities, and provides sufficient amenities to achieve excellence and offers exposure to the Professional education system.

KGR Institute of Technology and Management was sponsored by KESA Society.

The vision is driven by a spirit of social awareness, sincerity in offering the best education and striving for providing innovative solutions that serve the ever-changing community. To transform the enthusiastic, motivated and meritorious students into knowledgeable Managers who will meet the needs of society at local, national and global level is the mission of KGRITM.

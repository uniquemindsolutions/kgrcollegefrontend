Welcome to the Department of Pharmacy at KGR Institute of Technology and Management, Hyderabad. Since our establishment in 2011 under the visionary leadership of Dr. K. Govinda Reddy and the Karunamaya Educational & Social Awareness Society, we have been dedicated to delivering exceptional pharmacy education. Over the past Two Decades, we have made significant strides in building professional skills in community pharmacy, hospital pharmacy, clinical pharmacy, and industrial pharmacy. Our goal is to create a dynamic learning environment that nurtures excellence in pharmaceutical education and research, empowering our students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to thrive in the ever-evolving healthcare landscape. Our accomplished faculty members bring a wealth of experience from academia, research, and industry, and are passionate about mentoring our students and guiding them towards success. As the healthcare industry continues to advance, we emphasize hands-on learning, research, and practical training to ensure our graduates are well-prepared to meet future challenges. Whether you aspire to be a pharmacist, researcher, or industry leader, Our College provides the ideal platform and resources to turn your aspirations into reality.

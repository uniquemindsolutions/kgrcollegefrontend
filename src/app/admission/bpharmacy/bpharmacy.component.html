B.Pharmacy 100 seats 70 seats 30 seats

ADMISSIONS PROCESS:

  • The Convener TS-EAMCET directly makes convener quota admissions.
  • Convener quota is 70% of the total intake. The minimum qualification for admission to the first year of the B. Pharmacy course is a pass in the Intermediate (10 + 2) conducted by the board of Intermediate Education, Govt. of Telangana State/A.P or any other examination recognized as equivalent there to with Mathematics, Biology, Physics and Chemistry as optional subjects. 70 % of the seats are allotted based on the merit in the TSEAMCET.
  • Further details of the admission test can be obtained on TS-EAMCET and TSCHE
  • 30 % of the seats are earmarked for Management/NRI candidates.
  • The Institution makes management quota admission directly. On merit basis application from and prospectus and be had from the college office on all working days.

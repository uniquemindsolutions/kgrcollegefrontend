ADMISSIONS PROCESS:

  • The Convener PGECET directly makes convener quota admissions.
  • Convener quota is 70% of the total intake. Students who passed B.Pharmacy degree from any university recognized by UGC are eligible to apply for PGECET.
  • Further details of the admission test can be obtained on PGECET and TSCHE
  • The Institution makes management quota admission directly. On merit basis application from and prospectus and be had from the college office on all working days.

BACHELOR OF PHARMACY (B.Pharmacy)

B.Pharmacy 100 seats

Bachelor of Pharmacy is a four-year undergraduate programme that has been designed to enhance the skills of the students in terms of knowledge, communication, patient care, healthcare system organisations, development of professional identity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning. The Institute has introduced an innovative approach of Outcome Based Education (OBE) in the curriculum, befitting the needs of the day. The core pharmacy subjects include Pharmaceutics, Pharmacology, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmaceutical Analysis, and Pharmacognosy.

DURATION: 4 Years

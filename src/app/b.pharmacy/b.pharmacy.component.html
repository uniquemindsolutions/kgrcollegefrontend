BACHELOR OF PHARMACY (B.Pharmacy)

B.Pharmacy 100 seats

Bachelor of Pharmacy is a four-year undergraduate programme that has been designed to enhance the skills of the students in terms of knowledge, communication, patient care, healthcare system organisations, development of professional identity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning. The Institute has introduced an innovative approach of Outcome Based Education (OBE) in the curriculum, befitting the needs of the day. The core pharmacy subjects include Pharmaceutics, Pharmacology, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmaceutical Analysis, and Pharmacognosy.

DURATION: 4 Years

Admission to 2nd Year B.Pharmacy Courses through Lateral Entry Scheme Eligibility Criteria for Admission to 2nd year B.Pharmacy through Lateral Entry Scheme:

Students who have passed Diploma / B.Sc (Mathematics) can apply for admission to 2nd B.Pharmacy Professional Courses in respective branches according to their stream in diploma through Lateral Entry Scheme. Only those candidates who are passed with an aggregate marks of 50% in the final year/final semester of the Diploma programme.

Or

Who have obtained Diploma in the concerned branch of Engineering from State Board of Technical Education and training of Telangana State /Andhra Pradesh or any diploma recognized by the Government of Telangana as equivalent are eligible for admission for B. Tech. Degree courses in the branches of Engineering/Pharmacy corresponding to their Diploma and as permitted by the Government of Telangana from time to time.

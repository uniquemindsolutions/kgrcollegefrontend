LIBRARY

The Library is located in a spacious hall with 2500 Sq.ft Plinth area. The library has collection of all titles prescribed by AICTE and Osmania University. National and International Journals, Magazines and News papers are subscribed. It is also planned to digitalize the entire library. Book bank facility is available to all the students.

COMPUTER CENTER

Two Computer Centers are located in spacious halls with 3500 Sq.ft Plinth Area. At present 240 computers, 19inch TFT with 1:2 computer-student ratio, fully Wi-Fi Internet Enabled. Monitors with all Softwares prescribed by AICTE and Osmania University along with Servers, Colour Printers with scanners and latest copier machine available for the convenience of students and special revolving chairs for comfortable seating.

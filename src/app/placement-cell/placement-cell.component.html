The Training & Placement Cell

The Training & Placement Cell assists students in career planning and securing positions in the industry through internships and live projects. TPC will monitor MAM (My Activity Module) and equip students with enhanced employability skills during their master's program.

  • Every student will be registered in the TPC at the beginning of Ist semester and is expected to participate through out in activities of TPC till the end of Masters Program.
  • TPC will organize MAM (My Activity Module) and provide platform to the students to get trained in soft skills/communication skills, interview skills / group activities.
  • TPC will coordinate Finishing School Module.

Finishing School Module

This training module is focused at experiential learning covering Class room teaching, Role plays, Case Assignments, Group Discussions, Writing Assignments, seminar presentation, Mock interviews, and Internship projects facilitating the student to enhance his/her competency matrix to become employable. The Module is designed keeping Industry expectations in view.

  • Will spread over 2 semesters (semester III & IV) including preparatory classes for your Internship and final placement
  • About 100 hours of intensive personalized competency building
  • Workshops will be typically in the morning, evening or weekend times at our college center.
